Ashenda Tigrai
Festival 2022

Ashenda Festival 2022 will be celebrated in a special way in Mekelle, the capital city of the regional state of Tigrai in the presence of Heads of State and reknowned singers and other guests. You are all invited to this once in a year special event where girls and young women exclusively participate decorated by the cultural costume and the grass called “Ashenda”.
